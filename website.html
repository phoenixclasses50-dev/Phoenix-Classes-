ABOUT THE TEACHER

Rohit Yashwant Toraskar

With over 10 years of teaching experience and a passion for education, Rohit Toraskar founded Phoenix Classes at Bahe, Tal - Walwa, Dist - Sangli to provide quality Maths and Science coaching for Class 8, 9, and 10 students of Maharashtra Board.

His teaching philosophy is simple — every student can excel with the right guidance, consistent practice, and personal attention. He believes in making concepts clear rather than just completing the syllabus.

"My goal is not just to help students pass exams, but to build a strong foundation that serves them throughout their academic journey."
— Rohit Toraskar, Founder
